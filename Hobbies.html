What are Hobbies?

Hobbies are activities that people engage in regularly during their free time for pleasure, relaxation, and personal fulfillment. They can vary widely based on individual interests and can be both solitary or social. Here are some examples of my hobbies:

  1. Playing Sports like:
    • Basketball
    • Volleyball
    • Badminton
  2. Coding
  3. Sleeping
  4. Eating
  5. Enjoying books, magazines, or articles.
  6. Studying new languages and practicing speaking them.

These hobbies can provide a sense of achievement, relaxation, and enjoyment, contributing positively to one's overall well-being.
